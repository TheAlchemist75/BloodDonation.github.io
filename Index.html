Introduction

There’s no end to the benefits of donating blood for those who need it. According to the Red Cross, one donation can save as many as three lives, and someone in the India needs blood every two seconds. It turns out that donating blood doesn’t just benefit recipients. There are health benefits for donors, too, on top of the benefits that come from helping others. According to WHO, India suffers from an annual deficit of two million blood units, as ony 1% of India population donates blood each year.

“Give blood and keep the world-beating”.

The message highlights the essential contribution blood donors make to keeping the world pulsating by saving lives and improving others' health. It reinforces the global call for more people all over the world to donate blood regularly and contribute to better health.







Donate Now!

Blood donation is a vital part of worldwide healthcare. It is a constant challenge to find new and affordable ways to recruit and retain blood donors. With the donation of products, services and gift certificates, businesses and organizations can play an important role in helping us attract new blood donors. In-Kind contributions also help us thank current blood donors and encourage their continued blood donations as they are eligible.
